Besides, there is a page that leads to an enjoyable quiz which focuses on the directors films.

My Favorite Film Directors

My favorite film directors are Wong Kar Wai and Andrei Tarkovski. Wong Kar Wai is a master of sense. How he uses light, colours, sound, clothes and storyline mesmerize the viewer and touches hearts. Andrei Tarkovski is a master of soul. From the messages of films to his special technique, he is such a director that I can promise you his films will show you different realities of the world, give you a glimpse of stun and terrify you.

  • Master of Sense: Wong Kar Wai
  • Master of Soul: Andrei Tarkovsky
